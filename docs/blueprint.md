# **App Name**: Erica: Integrated Automation Experts

## Core Features:

- Automation Scorecard: Interactive assessment tool providing users with a maturity score and personalised recommendations. Results displayed immediately after submission.
- Lead-to-Sale System Building: Design and implementation of self-scaling systems that connect marketing, sales, and reporting, tailored to specific client needs. Specialising in automations between digital tools and CRMs.
- POPIA-Compliant Data Handling: Ensuring all forms and data integrations adhere to South African data privacy regulations, providing peace of mind for clients and their customers.
- SA Fintech Integrations: Seamless integration with local payment gateways such as PayFast and Ozow, facilitating frictionless transactions and improved customer experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary colour: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke trust, efficiency and a touch of South African flair (like the ocean). The teal is vibrant but professional.
- Background colour: Very light greenish-grey (#F0F8F8) for a clean and modern feel.
- Accent colour: A desaturated, dark, cool green (#468479), complementing the primary teal and providing contrast for call to actions and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, to promote readability, sleekness, and trustworthiness.
- Simple, modern line icons to represent key services and benefits, enhancing user comprehension.
- Clean and spacious layout, prioritizing clear communication and easy navigation. Emphasis on visual hierarchy to guide users through the content.
- Subtle transitions and animations to create a dynamic and engaging user experience, without being distracting.
